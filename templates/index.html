About FakeSnap

🎯 Our Mission +
FakeSnap is built to fight misinformation in the digital age. Our mission is to provide everyone with a free, fast, and reliable tool to verify news before sharing it. We believe that truth matters and that technology can help people make informed decisions.
⚙️ How It Works +
1 You enter a news article or upload an image
2 Our ML model analyzes the writing patterns
3 AI fact-checker verifies the content
4 You get a REAL, FAKE, or SUSPICIOUS verdict
🧠 How Model Was Trained +
Our model was trained on 100,981 verified news articles from ISOT and IFND datasets covering international and Indian news. We used TF-IDF vectorization with bigrams and Logistic Regression achieving 95.35% accuracy.
💻 Technology Stack +
FakeSnap is built using Python, Flask, Scikit-learn, NLTK, Pytesseract for OCR, Groq AI for fact-checking, MySQL for database, and a custom HTML/CSS/JS frontend.
👨‍💻 About The Team +
FakeSnap is developed as a college major project by a passionate developer focused on fighting misinformation using AI and machine learning technologies.
